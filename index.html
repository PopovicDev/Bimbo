ABOUT BIMBO

Bimbo had humble beginnings that quickly grew into a testament to craftsmanship and dedication. Founded by two lifelong friends, Brian and Michael, who shared a passion for working with wood and a vision to create bespoke, handcrafted furniture and carpentry solutions. With nothing but a small workshop and a few hand tools, they started taking on small projects, pouring their heart and soul into each piece they crafted.


As word of their exceptional carpentry skills and attention to detail spread, Bimbo gained a reputation for transforming ordinary wood into extraordinary creations. The company's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction was evident from the start, setting them on a path to becoming a respected and sought-after name in the world of carpentry and woodworking. Today, Bimbo stands as a symbol of artistry and craftsmanship, founded on a shared dream and a relentless pursuit of perfection.
